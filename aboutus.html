About Us


We became shopkeepers when Fur Coat No Knickers opened for business on Feb 28 2008. Before that we worked in West End theatre as costume supervisors. As far as we’re concerned, there’s little difference between making actors fit for the limelight and making our brides look stunning for their big day.

Between us we have over 15 years' experience on shows including The Lion King, Anything Goes, Daddy Cool, Joseph and His Amazing Technicolor Dreamcoat, Bad Girls the Musical and the National Theatre’s Once in a Lifetime and His Dark Materials. We also had the pleasure of working with the fabulous Kim Cattrall who needed fifties outfits for The Cryptogram at the Donmar Warehouse.

So you can be certain we know how to give you the star treatment.

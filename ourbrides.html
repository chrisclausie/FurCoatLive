Our Brides


A smooth guide to Tuscany.

Sunday, March 10, 2013
Vicki fell head first for this stunning fifties cream lace dress with a very full circle skirt. But she really wanted something strapless for her outdoor Tuscan wedding and when she first tried it, the dress had much more of an illusion bodice with a little sleeve. So we tucked the bodice in, did a bit of pinning, carefully draped the lace back on and explained we could make a new under-bodice with more structure than the original. Vicki saw past all the pins and trusted us. From there, we added a full petticoat, a thin silk sash with matching cover buttons detail and a flower birdcage veil. Vicki chose Rachel Simpson Eva's for her sunny day in the rolling hills and olive groves of Tuscany.

Vicki's dress also featured on Love My Dress.
Photos by Laura McCluskey.


Dear Emma and Laura, thank you so much for creating my beautiful dress, I absolutely loved wearing it on my wedding day. Coming to see you girls for my fittings was one of my favourite things to do as we planned our wedding this year. You girls are just so lovely and your shop is just an absolute delight! Thanks again for everything, Vicki xx

Veils


Our vintage-inspired veils are handmade in nylon tulle, mounted on a wire comb and bound off in satin.

Our veils are made to order and we have samples in the shop for you to try. The options available for our veils include: short fifties, fingertip or full-length. These can then be cut edge, satin-bound edge or a fine lace scalloped edge. To the comb you can then add a satin bow, a row of pearls or choose a stunning one-off vintage brooch.


A selection of vintage-inspired birdcage veils and pill box hats are also available. All of our veils are lovingly made by the FCNK girls.

Vintage Accessories


Our shop is full of beautiful, hand-picked vintage accessories.

It's a place to find unique evening bags and costume jewellery including bracelets, earrings and stunning necklaces. We have a wonderful range of brooches that can also be used as hair decorations, not to mention all kinds of tiaras and headpieces.

We also offer a choice of Maribu feather shrugs and boleros to complete your vintage look.


If you’ve already found your dress pop in and let us help you find the perfect accessories to complement it.

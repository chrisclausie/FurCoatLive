Vintage Frocks


Our shop is a relaxed place full of gorgeous vintage wedding dresses which we can fit, alter and customise to suit you and your personality. We love to help with your accessories, veils and we also stock Rachel Simpson's beautiful shoes. We mainly specialise in 1950s to 1960s vintage dresses. It's like a lucky dip, you never know what we might find or what you could fall in love with, so come and have a play. We'll also make you a bespoke petticoat to fit your dress which creates that real wow factor.


You can pop in any time to take a look at our accessories but you need to make an appointment to see and try on our collection of dresses.
